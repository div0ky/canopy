# Governance

Canopy currently uses a maintainer-led governance model.

Maintainers are responsible for roadmap direction, accepted architectural decisions, release
quality, security response, and stewardship of the package and repository namespaces. Decisions
should be made in public through issues, discussions, pull requests, and the manifesto whenever
security or privacy does not require confidentiality.

## Decision process

- Routine fixes and documentation changes are decided through pull-request review.
- Public API, lifecycle, persistence, security, packaging, or compatibility changes require an
  accepted decision record.
- Maintainers seek consensus. When consensus cannot be reached, the lead maintainer makes and
  documents the decision.
- Security embargoes may be handled privately until coordinated disclosure.

## Becoming a maintainer

Maintainers are invited based on sustained, technically sound contributions; respectful community
participation; sound judgment around compatibility and security; and demonstrated stewardship beyond
a single feature.

Governance will be revised before responsibility is distributed beyond the initial maintainer.
